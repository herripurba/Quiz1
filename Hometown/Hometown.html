SIDIKALANG

Sidikalang is my hometown dear. Sidikalang is the name of a sub-district in the districts of Dairi, North Sumatra and Sidikalang which is also the capital of Dairi Regency. Sidikalang is the ulayat area of ​​the Pakpak Suak Keppas Batak Tribe, but the majority of the Sidikalang tribe is the Toba Batak Tribe. In addition, there are also many people from the Karo, Javanese, Aceh, Minang and other tribes. Sidikalang consists of mountains and hills and the very cool air is one of the determining factors for the majority of the work of the Dairi people in general who are now farmers. One of the well-known superior commodities from Sidikalang is coffee. Sidikalang coffee is very famous for its delicious taste, not only domestically but almost all world coffee lovers admit it. Sidikalang coffee has also been able to compete with Brazilian coffee, which is one of the best coffees in the world.
