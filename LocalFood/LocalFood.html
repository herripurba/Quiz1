LOCAL FOOD

Typical food is a reflection of the culture and tradition of the area of origin of the food. Typical food is also a very famous food from the area which an outsider could find out about.
These are some of the specialties from Sidikalang

Coffee

One of the well-known superior commodities from Sidikalang is coffee. Sidikalang coffee is very famous for its delicious taste, not only domestically, but almost all world coffee lovers admit it. Sidikalang coffee has also been able to compete with Brazilian coffee, which is one of the best coffees in the world. Sidikalang coffee has a characteristic with a low acidity level, so people who have stomach acid problems can safely enjoy it. In addition, you will feel the aroma of chocolate between the bitter taste on the tongue.

Durian

Apart from being a coffee bean producing area, Sidikalang in Dairi Regency is also known as the center of the sweetest durian in North Sumatra. Besides its sweet taste, Durian Sidikalang also has a fairly large shape with a golden yellow flesh color. From the appearance alone, this durian is quite capable of arousing appetite. The sweet taste is perfect for pairing with Sidikalang's signature coffee. No wonder this Sidikalang Typical Durain is also one of the Sidikalang Specialties of Dairi Regency which is a pity to miss.

Pelleng

If ordinary people see this food, Pelleng might fall into the category as a unique culinary. However, for the local community in Sidikalang, Dairi Regency, Pelleng is quite a special and sacred food. This food is usually served during traditional ceremonies or major community events there. Pelleng is a typical Sidikalang food for Dairi Regency, which is made from cooked rice until it is hard-textured and processed with spices such as green onions, chilies, ginger, pepper, coconut milk, and turmeric. Served on a plate in a mountainous shape, this dish is usually served with chicken curry on the side.

Lappet

Jika Anda mengenal makanan bernama Awug, mungkin Anda tidak asing dengan makanan khas Sidikalang ini. Makanan yang masuk ke dalam kategori jajanan pasar ini dikenal dengan nama Lappet atau Lapet. Kue Lapet ini merupakan makanan khas kebanggaan dari masyarakat Sumatera Utara. Makanan Khas Sidikalang Kabupaten Dairi ini diolah menggunakan bahan beras yang disajikan di dalam daun pisang berbentuk kerucut. Makanan ini terasa sangat gurih untuk dengan tambahan gula merah dan parutan kelapa.

Ginaru

Ginaru adalah salah satu Makanan Khas Sidikalang Kabupaten Dairi Sumatera Utara yang bisa Anda temukan. Sekilas makanan ini tampak seperti kolak ubi. Namun yang jadi unik adalah bumbu yang digunakan dalam proses pembuatannya. Dalam proses pembuatan Ginaru, Anda akan menemukan bahan-bahan seperti kunyit, cabe, jahe, bawang gendera, serta cikala. Bumbu-bumbu tersebut dicambur ke dalam adonan ubi yang menghasilkan rasa gurih dan manis dalam saat yang bersamaan ketika masuk ke dalam mulut.

Pineapple Pakpak Bharat

Another famous produce that comes from Sidikalang in Dairi Regency is Pineapple Pakpak Bharat. Pineapple or pineapple fruit is a very superior agricultural product and is being promoted to be better known to the wider community from the government of North Sumatra. To find out this type of pineapple, you might be surprised by the color of its skin that is still green. But don't get me wrong, the meat inside has a sour and sweet taste that blends very refreshingly.
